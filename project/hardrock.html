Rock Legends

Learn about some of the greatest rock bands of all time

Queen

Queen is a British rock band formed in London in 1970. Their classic line-up consisted of Freddie Mercury (lead vocals, piano), Brian May (guitar, vocals), Roger Taylor (drums, vocals), and John Deacon (bass guitar).

Best Known For: "Bohemian Rhapsody", "We Will Rock You", "Another One Bites the Dust"

Guns N' Roses

Guns N' Roses is an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1985. The band's classic lineup, consisting of vocalist Axl Rose, lead guitarist Slash, rhythm guitarist Izzy Stradlin, bassist Duff McKagan, and drummer Steven Adler, achieved worldwide success in the late 1980s and early 1990s.

Best Known For: "Sweet Child o' Mine", "November Rain", "Welcome to the Jungle"

Bon Jovi

Bon Jovi is an American rock band formed in 1983 in Sayreville, New Jersey. The band consists of lead singer and namesake Jon Bon Jovi, pianist and keyboardist David Bryan, drummer Tico Torres, lead guitarist Phil X, and bassist Hugh McDonald.

Best Known For: "Livin' on a Prayer", "It's My Life", "Wanted Dead or Alive"
